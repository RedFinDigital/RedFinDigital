Mobile Website Update

The Good House Company is an award-winning developer of stunning luxury homes, single-builds and renovations.

The client asked us to update the mobile version of their pre-built website on the Wix platform. The website had been built for desktop PCs, but had not yet been edited to work on mobile devices. The structure of the mobile site was rebuilt and redesigned it to match the main desktop website.
