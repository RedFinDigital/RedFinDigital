Mobile Website Update

The Good House Company is an award-winning developer of stunning luxury homes, single-builds and renovations.

We are thrilled to share the news about our recently completed project - the update of the mobile site for The Good House Company. The Good House Company is a renowned builder of bespoke and luxurious houses, and they came to us with the challenge of improving the mobile experience of their pre-existing website, which was created on the Wix platform.

Our team of web design experts jumped into action and worked closely with the client to understand their needs and preferences. We carefully analyzed the existing website and identified areas for improvement, such as reordering content for better display on mobile devices and making the site more responsive and user-friendly.

The result is a stunning mobile site that showcases the beautiful houses built by The Good House Company in a way that is easy to navigate and enjoy on a mobile device. We used high-quality images and videos to highlight the features and amenities of each house, and we optimized the layout and design for a smooth and seamless browsing experience.

We are proud of the work we have done for The Good House Company, and we are confident that their customers will appreciate the enhanced mobile site. This project is a testament to our commitment to delivering exceptional web design services, and we are excited to continue working with The Good House Company and other clients in the future.
