Website development

Burger Boss is a local, family run burger restaurant in the heart of the UK. They pride themselves on using the finest ingredients, and making the best tasting burgers. Their food is guaranteed to be fresh, filling and satisfying.

The client requested a new new website, based on a pre-designed template. The site displays information on the restaurant, a showcase of their burgers and a table booking form. The company's pink branding is a prominent theme throughout the site.
