New Website

Holme Electrolysis is a beauty treatment center offering a range of services including electrolysis hair removal, nails, hand & foot care, lashes & brows, facials, and waxing treatments. The center prides itself on providing high-quality treatments to clients in a welcoming and professional environment.

We have recently completed the development of the website for Holme Electrolysis, a beauty treatment center.
Our team was tasked with bringing a third-party design to life and ensuring the website was fully functional. We worked closely with the client to make sure their needs were met and the final product met their expectations.

The website, built on WordPress, features a clean layout with straightforward navigation and clear calls to action. The range of beauty treatments offered by Holme Electrolysis, including electrolysis hair removal, nails, hand & foot care, lashes & brows, facials, and waxing treatments, are all featured on the site.

We are pleased with the outcome of this project and the positive feedback received from the client. This project demonstrates our ability to effectively implement a third-party design into a functional website using WordPress. We look forward to working on similar projects in the future.
