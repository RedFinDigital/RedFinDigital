Website and logo design

Main Nightingale Sporting Goods are one of the leading retailers of commercially available and bespoke alpine sports equipment. Their expert staff have extensive experience in the industry, offering a tailored service and the ability to source rare equipment.

We are proud to announce the completion of our latest project - the creation of a landing page for Main Nightingale Sporting Goods. Main Nightingale is one of the leading retailers of alpine sports equipment, offering a wide range of commercially available and bespoke products. Their expert staff has extensive experience in the industry, providing a tailored service and the ability to source rare equipment.

The client approached us with the need for a new logo, branding, and a coming soon landing page to be used as a placeholder until the launch of their new e-commerce website. We accepted the challenge and worked closely with the client to create a branding and logo that reflects their expertise and professionalism. The branding and logo incorporate a dark theme with green accents, which is carried throughout the website.

The resulting landing page is modern, engaging, and user-friendly. It features a sleek design that showcases the new branding and logo, as well as a countdown timer that counts down to the specified launch date of the e-commerce website. The landing page also includes a mailing list subscription form and a contact form, to allow customers to stay informed and get in touch with the company.

We are thrilled with the result of this project, and we believe that the landing page for Main Nightingale Sporting Goods will help them build anticipation and excitement for the upcoming launch of their e-commerce website.
